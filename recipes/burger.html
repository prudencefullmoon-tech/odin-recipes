Burger

Main page

Description

A sandwich made with a grilled or fried meat patty placed inside a soft bun.

Common toppings include lettuce, tomato, cheese, onions, and sauces.

It’s a fast-food favorite known for its juicy, satisfying taste.

Ingredients

  • Burger buns
  • Ground meat or plant-based patty
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices
  • Cheese slice
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard or mayonnaise
  • Pickles
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Form ground meat into patties
  2. Season with salt and pepper
  3. Heat a pan or grill
  4. Cook patties until done
  5. Toast burger buns
  6. Place patty on the bun
  7. Add cheese and toppings
  8. Add sauces and cover with the top bun
