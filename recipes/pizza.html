Pizza

Main page

Description

A popular Italian dish consisting of a flat dough base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings.

It’s baked in an oven until the crust is crispy and the cheese melts.

Loved worldwide for its endless flavor combinations.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Italian herbs (oregano, basil)
  • Vegetable or meat toppings
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Prepare or roll out pizza dough
  2. Preheat the oven
  3. Spread tomato sauce on the dough
  4. Add mozzarella cheese
  5. Add desired toppings
  6. Drizzle olive oil and sprinkle herbs
  7. Bake until crust is crispy and cheese melts
  8. Slice and serve
