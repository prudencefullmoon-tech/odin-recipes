Lasagna

Main page

Description

A classic Italian baked pasta dish made with layers of pasta sheets, meat or vegetable sauce, and creamy cheese.

It’s usually topped with melted cheese and baked until golden and bubbly.

Rich, hearty, and perfect for a comforting meal.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta sheets
  • Ground meat or vegetables
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta or béchamel sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic and onions
  • Olive oil
  • Italian herbs (basil, oregano)
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare the sauce
  2. Cook lasagna noodles
  3. Preheat the oven
  4. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese in a baking dish
  5. Repeat layers
  6. Top with cheese
  7. Bake until golden and bubbly
  8. Let it rest before serving
